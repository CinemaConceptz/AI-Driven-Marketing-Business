Tier Structure Definition
Verified Sound – Representation Platform
Overview

Verified Sound operates under a structured tier-based subscription model.

Each tier increases representation intensity while preserving professional outreach standards and infrastructure integrity.

Tiers differ in targeting depth, campaign frequency, and reporting access — not unrealistic promises.

Tier I – Foundation Representation
Purpose:

Structured entry-level representation for emerging electronic artists.

Includes:

• Professional EPK generation
• Strategic positioning summary
• Opportunity monitoring access
• Compatibility scoring
• Monthly targeted submission cycle
• Campaign activity dashboard
• Basic reporting metrics

Outreach Limits:

• Up to 5 qualified submissions per month
• Controlled follow-up cycle

Ideal For:

Emerging artists building initial industry exposure.

Tier II – Active Representation
Purpose:

Increased targeting intensity and structured campaign cadence.

Includes:

• Everything in Tier I
• Enhanced compatibility filtering
• Bi-weekly submission cycles
• Advanced reporting metrics
• Campaign refinement recommendations
• Priority support

Outreach Limits:

• Up to 12 qualified submissions per month
• Structured follow-up tracking

Ideal For:

Artists actively releasing music and seeking consistent label engagement.

Tier III – Strategic Placement Campaign
Purpose:

High-intensity representation campaign within defined genres.

Includes:

• Everything in Tier II
• Weekly submission cycles
• Expanded opportunity scanning
• Advanced compatibility scoring
• Performance analytics dashboard
• Campaign optimization insights
• Priority targeting queue

Outreach Limits:

• Up to 20 qualified submissions per month
• Structured follow-up escalation cycle

Ideal For:

Established independent artists with strong catalog quality seeking accelerated exposure.

Outreach Governance Across All Tiers

Regardless of tier:

• No indiscriminate mass submissions
• Compatibility threshold must be met
• Daily submission caps enforced
• Anti-spam compliance maintained
• Label submission rules strictly respected

Upgrade & Downgrade Policy

• Artists may upgrade at any time
• Downgrades apply at next billing cycle
• Outreach limits reset monthly
• Campaign data remains accessible across tiers

Infrastructure Safeguards

To preserve industry credibility:

• Submission volume may be reduced if compatibility score is insufficient
• Labels flagged as inactive are removed
• Outreach pauses if system detects quality concerns

Quality is prioritized over quantity.
