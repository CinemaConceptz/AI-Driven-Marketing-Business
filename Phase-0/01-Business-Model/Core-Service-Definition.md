Core Service Definition
Verified Sound – Representation Platform
Service Classification

Verified Sound operates as a structured digital representation platform providing artist development support and strategic demo targeting within defined electronic music genres.

The platform is subscription-based and does not operate as a traditional talent agency or management firm.

What Verified Sound Provides

Verified Sound provides:

• Structured artist profiling
• Professional electronic press kit generation
• Strategic positioning guidance
• Active label opportunity monitoring
• Compatibility-based label matching
• Controlled demo outreach execution
• Campaign tracking and reporting
• Ongoing opportunity intelligence

Representation is systematic and structured, not informal or random.

What Verified Sound Does NOT Provide

Verified Sound does not:

• Guarantee record deals
• Guarantee placements
• Guarantee responses from labels
• Act as exclusive artist management
• Negotiate contracts
• Collect commissions on artist earnings
• Provide legal representation
• Provide financial advisory services

The platform facilitates structured access and outreach — not contractual negotiations.

Artist Responsibilities

Artists are responsible for:

• Submitting original, legally owned material
• Confirming rights ownership
• Providing accurate information during intake
• Reviewing and approving outreach materials
• Maintaining professional conduct

Artists must ensure their material does not infringe copyright, trademark, or contractual obligations.

Platform Responsibilities

Verified Sound is responsible for:

• Maintaining a structured opportunity database
• Executing outreach within defined volume limits
• Preserving submission quality standards
• Protecting user data
• Maintaining ethical outreach practices
• Avoiding indiscriminate mass submissions

Outreach is executed only when compatibility standards are met.

Outreach Governance

Outreach activity is governed by:

• Subscription tier limitations
• Daily and monthly submission caps
• Genre compatibility thresholds
• Anti-spam compliance safeguards
• Quality scoring systems

The platform prioritizes strategic alignment over volume.

Representation Scope

Representation is non-exclusive.

Artists may pursue independent submissions or additional representation simultaneously.

Verified Sound operates as a supplemental structured representation infrastructure.

Service Modification Clause

Service features, targeting models, and outreach systems may evolve as infrastructure improves, provided that changes do not violate subscription terms or misrepresent service scope.

Ethical Standards

Verified Sound adheres to:

• Transparent communication
• No false placement promises
• No inflated performance claims
• Responsible outreach behavior
• Respect for label submission policies

The platform is designed to preserve long-term industry credibility.
